About Us

"Marine Expertise, Unmatched Reliability"



Triton Marine Solutions (TMS) is a premier marine services and maintenance company focused on enhancing the operational efficiency and safety of jack-up barges and vessels in the oil and gas industry. With a strong presence in key markets, particularly with industry leaders like Saudi ARAMCO and ADNOC, we specialize in the maintenance and inspection of jacking systems critical for the stability and success of offshore operations.
